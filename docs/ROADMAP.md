# Portfolio Development Roadmap

## Objective

Build a production-quality engineering portfolio incrementally, ensuring every milestone results in a stable, deployable application.

Development follows a feature-first approach rather than attempting to build the entire application at once.

# Definition of Success

The roadmap is complete when the portfolio:

- Reflects the engineering philosophy defined in PROJECT_CONTEXT.md
- Follows the DESIGN_SYSTEM.md
- Adheres to the ARCHITECTURE.md
- Meets all PRD acceptance criteria
- Is deployed and publicly accessible
- Demonstrates production-level engineering quality
